EMPATHY BUILDER

Financial Tools to Empower and Transform Lives

Why Empathy Builder?

Without a way to rebuild financially, survivors typically won't leave an abuser. If they do, they risk homelessness, losing custody of their children, or having to return to their abuser.

source: FreeFrom.org


Financial insecurity is the No. 1 reason survivors of domestic violence remain in or return to abuse. In order to break the cycle of violence, we must remove obstacles to financial security for survivors. To that end, Freefrom.org is developing an interactive calculator for their website. It will serve survivors to enable them to better understand the costs of leaving and staying safe. The calculator will include prompts to help users understand and identify the relevant costs. This project will not only help survivors and raise awareness of the costs of domestic violence, but will also capture valuable data that will help FreeFrom drive positive policy change!

What Is Empathy Builder?

Empathy Builder is a new financial toolkit designed to empower survivors of domestic violence to secure their financial stability. EMPATHY BUILDER features include budget planner, relocation cost tor with integrated geo-location APIs to predict cost of living expenses, and optional data storage to save your searches. Protecting client's privacy and security are top priority. EMPATHY BUILDER is designed to protect our client's confidentiality.

MONEY MANAGEMENT

RELOCATION COST ESTIMATOR

Relocating? Know the cost before you go. With Empathy Builder, you can estimate cost of living expenses in Denver, Utah or Peoria and compare the costs.

FINANCIAL ROADMAP

Financial stability is not a destination but a journey of a million steps. Empathy Builder, provides safe and confidential financial management.
